Frequently Asked Questions

Is business name numerology scientifically proven?

While numerology isn't recognized as a hard science, it's been used for thousands of years across multiple cultures. Many successful business leaders use numerology as one tool among many for strategic decision-making. The patterns observed in successful business names are often too consistent to dismiss as mere coincidence.

Should I change my business name if the numerology is bad?

Not necessarily. Our calculator provides balancing strategies for challenging numbers. Sometimes small adjustments (like adding or removing a letter) can significantly improve the energy without requiring a complete rebrand.

How accurate is this calculator?

Our calculator uses the authentic Pythagorean numerology system, the same method used by professional numerologists. For common names, it's extremely accurate. For very unique names or those with special characters, we recommend consulting a professional numerologist for deeper analysis.

Do I need to use my legal business name?

For most accurate results, use the name that appears on your official registration. However, you can also test your "doing business as" (DBA) name or nickname to see how the energy compares.
